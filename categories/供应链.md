## 1. 供应链安全库存计算器

**Prompt：**
```
Calculate safety stock levels for my SKUs based on:

SKU data: [粘贴SKU列表含历史需求]
Lead time: [平均交货天数]
Service level target: [95%/97%/99%]
Demand variability: [描述需求波动特征]
Current stock levels: [当前库存]

For each SKU, calculate:
1. Safety stock using standard deviation method
2. Reorder point (safety stock + lead time demand)
3. Cycle stock vs safety stock ratio
4. Stockout probability at current levels
5. Cost of carrying safety stock vs cost of stockouts
6. Recommendations for high-variability items
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — AI Prompts for Supply Chain 2026
> 用法：上传SKU数据，AI 自动计算安全库存和再订货点，减少缺货风险。

