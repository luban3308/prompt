## 1. 商业模式画布生成

**Prompt：**
```
Generate a Business Model Canvas for:

Business idea: [描述]
Industry: [行业]
Target customer: [目标客户]

Fill in all 9 building blocks:
1. Customer Segments
2. Value Propositions
3. Channels
4. Customer Relationships
5. Revenue Streams
6. Key Resources
7. Key Activities
8. Key Partnerships
9. Cost Structure

For each block, provide specific details and assumptions.
Flag any block that needs more research.
```
> 来源：AiledGrowth / SurePrompts — Business 2026
> 用法：从商业想法生成完整的九宫格商业模式画布。

## 2. 竞品分析矩阵

**Prompt：**
```
Perform a competitive analysis for:

Our product: [产品]
Competitors: [竞品列表]
Market: [市场/行业]

For each competitor, analyze:
1. Product features (feature comparison matrix)
2. Pricing model
3. Target audience
4. Strengths (what they do better)
5. Weaknesses (gaps we can exploit)
6. Market positioning
7. Recent moves (product launches, acquisitions)

Summary: our competitive advantage and recommended strategy.
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Competitive Analysis 2026
> 用法：完整竞品分析，含特征对比矩阵和竞争策略建议。

## 3. 定价策略分析

**Prompt：**
```
Help me develop a pricing strategy for:

Product: [产品描述]
Cost: [成本结构]
Competitors pricing: [竞品定价]
Target market: [目标市场]
Value metric: [按什么收费]

Analyze:
1. Pricing models to consider (subscription/one-time/freemium/tiered)
2. Recommended price points with rationale
3. Psychology of each price point
4. Discount/bundle strategy
5. Enterprise pricing approach
6. Price sensitivity factors
7. Testing methodology for pricing
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Business Prompts 2026
> 用法：定价策略分析，含心理定价和折扣策略。

## 4. 风险登记表

**Prompt：**
```
Create a risk register for:

Project: [项目名称]
Scope: [项目范围]
Stakeholders: [关键干系人]

Identify and assess risks:

Risk | Probability (1-5) | Impact (1-5) | Risk Score | Mitigation Strategy | Contingency Plan | Owner

For each risk:
- Describe the risk clearly
- Rate probability and impact
- Calculate risk score (P × I)
- Primary mitigation
- Backup plan if mitigation fails
- Assign owner
```
> 来源：IBM 2026 Guide to Prompt Engineering
> 用法：生成完整风险登记表，含概率、影响评分和应对策略。

## 5. 数据解读报告

**Prompt：**
```
Interpret this data and provide actionable insights:

Data: [粘贴数据表格或描述]
Context: [数据背景]
Question: [想通过数据回答的问题]

Provide:
1. Key findings (top 3-5)
2. Trends and patterns
3. Statistical significance (where applicable)
4. Data quality caveats
5. Actionable recommendations
6. What additional data would help

Avoid: over-interpreting noise, suggesting causality from correlation.
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Data Interpretation 2026
> 用法：数据解读模板，含统计显著性和数据质量警告。

## 6. 投资人更新邮件

**Prompt：**
```
Write an investor update email:

Company name: [公司名]
Period: [报告周期]
Key metrics: [关键数据]
Milestones achieved: [里程碑]
Challenges: [面临的挑战]
Next quarter goals: [下季度目标]
Ask (if any): [需要什么帮助]

Structure:
1. Quick summary (3 bullet points)
2. Key metrics table (current vs previous vs target)
3. Narrative (what happened and why)
4. Challenges and how we're addressing them
5. Ask / how they can help
6. Forward-looking outlook
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Investor Update 2026
> 用法：投资人更新邮件模板，快速总结+数据表格+叙事。

## 7. 财务模型基础

**Prompt：**
```
Build a simple financial model for:

Business type: [业务类型]
Revenue model: [收入模式]
Cost structure: [成本结构]
Growth assumptions: [增长假设]
Time horizon: [X年]

Generate:
1. Revenue projection (monthly, by revenue stream)
2. Cost breakdown (fixed, variable, COGS)
3. Gross margin analysis
4. Operating expenses
5. Cash flow projection
6. Unit economics (CAC, LTV, payback period)
7. Key metrics dashboard
8. Breakeven analysis

Flag key assumptions and their sensitivity.
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Financial Analysis 2026
> 用法：基础的财务模型搭建，含单位经济模型和盈亏平衡分析。

## 8. 反向思维：找出方案的致命缺陷
> 📅 2026-07-05

**Prompt：**
```
I'm considering [方案/计划]. Act as an expert critic and argue the 5 best reasons why this decision could be a mistake. Be specific and rigorous. For each reason: (1) describe the potential failure scenario, (2) estimate likelihood (low/med/high), (3) suggest how to mitigate this risk, (4) what early warning signs to watch for. After presenting the critique, provide a balanced assessment.
```
> 来源：nachoconesa.com — Decision Making (2026)
> 用法：反向思维法——让 AI 扮演批评家找出方案的致命缺陷，避免决策偏见。

## 9. 价值链分析
> 📅 2026-07-06

**Prompt：**
```
Conduct a value chain analysis for:

Company: [公司名称]
Industry: [行业]
Core product/service: [核心产品]

Analyze each primary activity:
1. Inbound logistics
2. Operations
3. Outbound logistics
4. Marketing & sales
5. Service

And each support activity:
6. Firm infrastructure
7. HR management
8. Technology development
9. Procurement

For each: current state assessment, cost drivers, differentiation opportunities, and recommended improvements.
```
> 来源：AI Academy / SurePrompts — Strategy Frameworks (2026)
> 用法：基于 Porter 价值链模型的分析框架。

## 10. 最小可行产品（MVP）定义
> 📅 2026-07-06

**Prompt：**
```
Define the MVP for:

Product idea: [产品概念]
Target users: [目标用户]
Core problem: [核心问题]
Team size: [团队规模]
Timeline: [期望上线时间]
Technical constraints: [技术限制]

Define:
1. Must-have features (what makes it viable, not just minimal)
2. Nice-to-have (phase 2)
3. Out of scope (explicitly excluded)
4. Success criteria (what proves the hypothesis)
5. Build-measure-learn loop design
6. Riskiest assumption and how to test it first
7. Estimated build effort per feature (hours/days)
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Product Development (2026)
> 用法：MVP 定义框架，区分必选/可选/排除功能。

## 11. 融资演讲稿大纲
> 📅 2026-07-06

**Prompt：**
```
Outline a pitch deck for:

Company: [公司名]
Stage: [种子轮/A轮/B轮]
Raise amount: [融资金额]
Product: [产品一句话描述]
Traction: [关键数据]
Competition: [竞争格局]

Slide-by-slide outline:
1. Title (company name, tagline, presenter info)
2. Problem (the pain, who feels it, how big)
3. Solution (product demo / value prop)
4. Market size (TAM, SAM, SOM with sources)
5. Business model (how you make money)
6. Traction (key metrics, growth, logos)
7. Product roadmap (now, next, later)
8. Competition (2x2 matrix, our advantage)
9. Team (key members, relevant experience)
10. Financials (projections, use of funds)
11. Ask (how much, what it buys, milestones)

For each slide: key message, visual suggestion, speaker notes.
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Fundraising (2026)
> 用法：融资演讲的逐页大纲，含每页核心信息和演讲备注。

## 12. 投资人尽职调查清单
> 📅 2026-07-06

**Prompt：**
```
Create an investor due diligence checklist for:

Company: [公司名]
Stage: [种子轮/A轮]
Industry: [行业]
Already prepared: [已有材料]

Generate a checklist organized by:
1. Corporate (incorporation, cap table, IP assignments)
2. Financial (financial statements, projections, unit economics)
3. Product (architecture, roadmap, tech stack)
4. Market (TAM analysis, competitive landscape)
5. Team (org chart, key hire needs, compensation)
6. Legal (contracts, regulatory compliance, IP)
7. Sales (pipeline, churn, LTV/CAC, sales process)

For each item: what to prepare, common investor questions, red flags to watch for.
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Due Diligence (2026)
> 用法：融资尽调清单，涵盖公司/财务/产品/法务等全维度。
