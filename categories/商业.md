## 1. 商业模式画布生成

**Prompt：**
```
Generate a Business Model Canvas for:

Business idea: [描述]
Industry: [行业]
Target customer: [目标客户]

Fill in all 9 building blocks:
1. Customer Segments
2. Value Propositions
3. Channels
4. Customer Relationships
5. Revenue Streams
6. Key Resources
7. Key Activities
8. Key Partnerships
9. Cost Structure

For each block, provide specific details and assumptions.
Flag any block that needs more research.
```
> 来源：AiledGrowth / SurePrompts — Business 2026
> 用法：从商业想法生成完整的九宫格商业模式画布。

## 4. 风险登记表


## 13. 商业提示词质量自评（BPQR）

**Prompt：**
```
Evaluate your output against these criteria (score 0-2 each, target >=8/10):

1. Factual Accuracy — Matches provided sources; no invented data.
2. Relevance — Directly serves the stated audience and goal.
3. Clarity — Short sentences; plain English; unambiguous.
4. Structure & Format — Output exactly as specified.
5. Constraints Compliance — Tone, length, style followed.
6. Verifiability — Citations or references included where asked.
7. Safety & Privacy — No PII leakage or policy violations.

If total < 8/10, improve the output and resubmit.
```
> 来源：GEND.CO — Business Prompt Quality Rubric (2026)
> 用法：让AI输出自评分数，低于阈值自动迭代改进，适合生产环境中的QA流程。

## 16. 增长实验生成器

**Prompt：**
```
Generate a backlog of growth experiments for [产品].

Current focus area: [优化方向]
Key metric to improve: [核心指标]
Past experiments: [已做过的实验]
Resources: [团队/时间/预算]

Generate 15 experiment ideas, each with:
1. Hypothesis (if X → Y because Z)
2. Experiment design (what to change, how to measure)
3. ICE score (Impact × Confidence × Ease, each 1-10)
4. Minimum success criteria
5. Duration for statistical significance
6. Risk level

Rank by ICE score. Flag experiments doable in < 1 week.
```
> 来源：AiledGrowth — Growth Experiment Generator (2026)
> 用法：15个实验想法带ICE评分，标注一周内可完成的快速实验。

## 19. 客户流失预警模型

**Prompt：**
```
Help me build a customer churn detection framework.

Business model: [SaaS/订阅/电商]
Customer lifecycle: [典型客户生命周期]
Available customer data: [使用频率/支持工单/NPS分数/付款记录/账户活动]
Current churn rate: [当前流失率]
High-value customer definition: [高价值客户标准]

Design a churn prediction approach:

1. Leading indicators — top 5 behavioral signals that precede churn
2. Risk scoring — how to calculate a churn risk score for each customer
3. Segmentation by risk level — green / yellow / red categories
4. At-risk segment deep dive — what do these customers have in common?
5. Retention playbook — specific intervention per risk level and segment
6. Trigger events — what events (e.g., support ticket volume drop) should auto-flag
7. Measurement — how to track if interventions are working
8. Feedback loop — how to incorporate outcomes to improve the model

Design a retention email/action sequence for each risk level.
```
> 📅 2026-07-12
> 来源：Customer Success / AI Business (2026)
> 用法：客户流失预警框架——从行为信号到主动干预，帮你在客户流失前主动行动。

## 20. 价格敏感度测试设计

**Prompt：**
```
Design a pricing experiment for our product.

Product: [产品描述]
Current price: [当前定价]
Target market: [目标市场]
Customer segment: [细分客户群体]
Pricing model: [一次性/订阅/按使用量]

Design the experiment:

1. Van Westendorp price sensitivity meter — define acceptable price range
2. Conjoint analysis design — attribute trade-offs (price vs features)
3. Tiered pricing structure — propose 3 pricing tiers with feature differentiation
4. Price anchoring strategy — which tier is the decoy?
5. Willingness-to-pay survey — question design to avoid bias
6. A/B test design — how to test price points without alienating customers
7. Grandfathering strategy — how to handle existing customers on old pricing
8. Metrics to track — conversion rate, average revenue per user, churn

Add ethical pricing considerations: avoid dark patterns, transparent communication.
```
> 📅 2026-07-12
> 来源：Pricing Strategy / AI Business Prompts (2026)
> 用法：科学定价实验设计——Van Westendorp法、联合分析、锚定效应，避免拍脑袋定价。
