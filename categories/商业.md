6. Trigger events — what events (e.g., support ticket volume drop) should auto-flag

## 20. 价格敏感度测试设计

**Prompt：**
```
Design a pricing experiment for our product.

Product: [产品描述]
Current price: [当前定价]
Target market: [目标市场]
Customer segment: [细分客户群体]
Pricing model: [一次性/订阅/按使用量]

Design the experiment:

1. Van Westendorp price sensitivity meter — define acceptable price range
2. Conjoint analysis design — attribute trade-offs (price vs features)
3. Tiered pricing structure — propose 3 pricing tiers with feature differentiation
4. Price anchoring strategy — which tier is the decoy?
5. Willingness-to-pay survey — question design to avoid bias
6. A/B test design — how to test price points without alienating customers
7. Grandfathering strategy — how to handle existing customers on old pricing
8. Metrics to track — conversion rate, average revenue per user, churn

Add ethical pricing considerations: avoid dark patterns, transparent communication.
```
> 📅 2026-07-12
> 来源：Pricing Strategy / AI Business Prompts (2026)
> 用法：科学定价实验设计——Van Westendorp法、联合分析、锚定效应，避免拍脑袋定价。
