## 1. 商业模式画布生成

**Prompt：**
```
Generate a Business Model Canvas for:

Business idea: [描述]
Industry: [行业]
Target customer: [目标客户]

Fill in all 9 building blocks:
1. Customer Segments
2. Value Propositions
3. Channels
4. Customer Relationships
5. Revenue Streams
6. Key Resources
7. Key Activities
8. Key Partnerships
9. Cost Structure

For each block, provide specific details and assumptions.
Flag any block that needs more research.
```
> 来源：AiledGrowth / SurePrompts — Business 2026
> 用法：从商业想法生成完整的九宫格商业模式画布。

## 2. 竞品分析矩阵

**Prompt：**
```
Perform a competitive analysis for:

Our product: [产品]
Competitors: [竞品列表]
Market: [市场/行业]

For each competitor, analyze:
1. Product features (feature comparison matrix)
2. Pricing model
3. Target audience
4. Strengths (what they do better)
5. Weaknesses (gaps we can exploit)
6. Market positioning
7. Recent moves (product launches, acquisitions)

Summary: our competitive advantage and recommended strategy.
```

## 4. 风险登记表

**Prompt：**
```
Create a risk register for:

Project: [项目名称]
Scope: [项目范围]
Stakeholders: [关键干系人]

Identify and assess risks:

Risk | Probability (1-5) | Impact (1-5) | Risk Score | Mitigation Strategy | Contingency Plan | Owner

For each risk:
- Describe the risk clearly
- Rate probability and impact
- Calculate risk score (P × I)
- Primary mitigation
- Backup plan if mitigation fails
- Assign owner
```
> 来源：IBM 2026 Guide to Prompt Engineering
> 用法：生成完整风险登记表，含概率、影响评分和应对策略。

## 5. 数据解读报告

Business type: [业务类型]

## 8. 反向思维：找出方案的致命缺陷
> 📅 2026-07-05

**Prompt：**
```
I'm considering [方案/计划]. Act as an expert critic and argue the 5 best reasons why this decision could be a mistake. Be specific and rigorous. For each reason: (1) describe the potential failure scenario, (2) estimate likelihood (low/med/high), (3) suggest how to mitigate this risk, (4) what early warning signs to watch for. After presenting the critique, provide a balanced assessment.
```
> 来源：nachoconesa.com — Decision Making (2026)
> 用法：反向思维法——让 AI 扮演批评家找出方案的致命缺陷，避免决策偏见。

## 10. 最小可行产品（MVP）定义
> 📅 2026-07-06

**Prompt：**
```
Define the MVP for:

Product idea: [产品概念]
Target users: [目标用户]
Core problem: [核心问题]
Team size: [团队规模]
Timeline: [期望上线时间]
Technical constraints: [技术限制]

Define:
1. Must-have features (what makes it viable, not just minimal)
2. Nice-to-have (phase 2)
3. Out of scope (explicitly excluded)
4. Success criteria (what proves the hypothesis)
5. Build-measure-learn loop design
6. Riskiest assumption and how to test it first
7. Estimated build effort per feature (hours/days)
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Product Development (2026)
> 用法：MVP 定义框架，区分必选/可选/排除功能。

## 11. 融资演讲稿大纲
> 📅 2026-07-06

**Prompt：**
```
Outline a pitch deck for:

Company: [公司名]
Stage: [种子轮/A轮/B轮]
Raise amount: [融资金额]
Product: [产品一句话描述]
Traction: [关键数据]
Competition: [竞争格局]

Slide-by-slide outline:
1. Title (company name, tagline, presenter info)
2. Problem (the pain, who feels it, how big)
3. Solution (product demo / value prop)
4. Market size (TAM, SAM, SOM with sources)
5. Business model (how you make money)
6. Traction (key metrics, growth, logos)
7. Product roadmap (now, next, later)
8. Competition (2x2 matrix, our advantage)
9. Team (key members, relevant experience)
10. Financials (projections, use of funds)
11. Ask (how much, what it buys, milestones)

For each slide: key message, visual suggestion, speaker notes.
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — Fundraising (2026)
> 用法：融资演讲的逐页大纲，含每页核心信息和演讲备注。

## 13. 商业提示词质量自评（BPQR）

**Prompt：**
```
Evaluate your output against these criteria (score 0-2 each, target >=8/10):

1. Factual Accuracy — Matches provided sources; no invented data.
2. Relevance — Directly serves the stated audience and goal.
3. Clarity — Short sentences; plain English; unambiguous.
4. Structure & Format — Output exactly as specified.
5. Constraints Compliance — Tone, length, style followed.
6. Verifiability — Citations or references included where asked.
7. Safety & Privacy — No PII leakage or policy violations.

If total < 8/10, improve the output and resubmit.
```
> 来源：GEND.CO — Business Prompt Quality Rubric (2026)
> 用法：让AI输出自评分数，低于阈值自动迭代改进，适合生产环境中的QA流程。

## 14. 职业转型简历定制

**Prompt：**
```
Tailor my resume for a career transition:

Current role: [当前职位]
Target role: [目标职位]
Target industry: [目标行业]
Transferable skills: [可迁移技能]
Gap to bridge: [需要补充的领域]

My current resume: [粘贴简历]
Job description for target role: [粘贴工作描述]

Rewrite bullet points to:
1. Emphasize transferable skills relevant to target role
2. Use keywords from the job description
3. Frame past achievements in context of new industry
4. Quantify wherever possible
5. Order skills by relevance to target, not current role
```
> 来源：HRLens.io / Resumly — AI Career Change Prompts (2026)
> 用法：职业转型定制简历，重点是可迁移技能的重新框架和关键词匹配。

## 15. 销售对战卡

**Prompt：**
```
Generate a sales battlecard for competing against [竞品].

Our product: [产品]
Competitor: [竞品名称]
Common evaluation criteria: [客户常见对比维度]

For each dimension, provide:
1. Our honest position
2. Competitor's likely claim
3. Our response (truthful counter-argument)
4. Proof point (case study or data)
5. Landmine questions — questions to ask that expose their weaknesses

Also include:
- Top 3 objections when competing against them (with responses)
- When to compete on price vs value vs features
- When to disqualify (not worth chasing)
```
> 来源：AiledGrowth — Sales Battlecard Generator (2026)
> 用法：结构化对战卡含"地雷问题"——能暴露竞品弱点的提问。

## 16. 增长实验生成器

**Prompt：**
```
Generate a backlog of growth experiments for [产品].

Current focus area: [优化方向]
Key metric to improve: [核心指标]
Past experiments: [已做过的实验]
Resources: [团队/时间/预算]

Generate 15 experiment ideas, each with:
1. Hypothesis (if X → Y because Z)
2. Experiment design (what to change, how to measure)
3. ICE score (Impact × Confidence × Ease, each 1-10)
4. Minimum success criteria
5. Duration for statistical significance
6. Risk level

Rank by ICE score. Flag experiments doable in < 1 week.
```
> 来源：AiledGrowth — Growth Experiment Generator (2026)
> 用法：15个实验想法带ICE评分，标注一周内可完成的快速实验。
