## 1. 复杂概念速通（费曼学习法）

**Prompt：**
```
Explain [概念] to me using the Feynman Technique.

First, explain it as if I'm a bright 12-year-old (simple analogies, no jargon).
Then, explain it at an undergraduate level (key theories, models).
Finally, at a graduate level (current debates,open questions).

For each level, identify the most common misconceptions.
```
> 来源：AI Academy — Learning & Self-Improvement 2026
> 用法：三层递进解释法，从简单到专业，附带常见误解。

## 4. Socratic 问答学习

**Prompt：**
```
Teach me about [主题] using the Socratic method.

Do not give me answers directly. Instead:
1. Ask me a series of questions that lead to understanding
2. Based on my answers, probe deeper
3. Point out contradictions in my thinking
4. Guide me toward the correct understanding
5. Only explain after I've attempted to reason through

Start with the most fundamental question about this topic.
```
> 来源：AI Academy / Prompt Engineering Guide 2026
> 用法：苏格拉底式教学法：通过提问引导你自己发现答案。

```

- Wait for my answer before continuing.

- When I get something right, explain why it's right — don't just say "correct."
```

## 32. 间隔重复备考计划

**Prompt：**
```
Create a spaced repetition study plan for my upcoming exam.

Exam: [考试名称]
Date: [考试日期]
Topics to cover: [主题列表]
My current proficiency per topic (1-5): [各主题当前掌握程度]
Available study time per day: [每天可学习时间]
Preferred study method: [阅读/做题/视频/闪卡]

Using Ebbinghaus Forgetting Curve principles:

1. Generate a day-by-day study calendar from today to exam day
2. For each day: what topics to review (based on when they were last studied)
3. Schedule reviews at: 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, 14 days, 30 days after first learning
4. Prioritize weak topics (lower proficiency) for more frequent review
5. Mix old and new topics each session to strengthen connections
6. Include self-testing days (active recall, no notes)
7. Leave the last 2 days for light review only (consolidation, not new material)

Provide the calendar as a table showing: Day | Topics to Learn | Topics to Review | Session Type (Learn/Review/Test)
```
> 📅 2026-07-12
> 来源：StudyTech / AI Learning Prompts (2026)
> 用法：基于艾宾浩斯遗忘曲线的个性化备考计划，根据当前掌握程度动态安排复习频率。
