## 1. 成功标准提示法（Success Criteria Prompting）

**Prompt：**
```
I need you to [任务描述].

Before you answer, define success criteria for this task. Tell me what a good answer looks like:
- Who is the audience and what do they care about?
- What format should the answer take?
- What specific quality bar should the response meet?
- What would make the output unusable?

Then produce your answer, and after it, score yourself against your own criteria from 1-10.
```
> 来源：Craig Does AI — Success Criteria Prompting (YouTube 2026)
> 用法：先让AI定义"好"的标准再输出答案，避免"make this better"式的模糊指令。适合需要高质量输出的场景。

## 2. CRAFT 提示框架（Context-Role-Action-Format-Tone）

**Prompt：**
```
**Context:** [背景信息 — 受众、场景、约束条件]
**Role:** [AI应扮演的角色 — 什么专业背景]
**Action:** [具体的任务描述]
**Format:** [输出格式要求 — 表格/段落/要点/JSON]
**Tone:** [语气风格 — 专业/亲切/幽默/简明]

请严格按以上结构执行。
```
> 来源：SurePrompts — The 10 Best AI Prompt Frameworks (2026)
> 用法：CRAFT 框架覆盖提示词的五个核心要素，适合80%的通用任务。默认框架首选。

## 6. 商业提示词质量自评（BPQR）

**Prompt：**
```
Evaluate your output against these criteria (score 0-2 each, target >=8/10):

1. Factual Accuracy — Matches provided sources; no invented data.
2. Relevance — Directly serves the stated audience and goal.
3. Clarity — Short sentences; plain English; unambiguous.
4. Structure & Format — Output exactly as specified.
5. Constraints Compliance — Tone, length, style followed.
6. Verifiability — Citations or references included where asked.
7. Safety & Privacy — No PII leakage or policy violations.

If total < 8/10, improve the output and resubmit.
```
> 来源：GEND.CO — Business Prompt Quality Rubric (2026)
> 用法：让AI输出自评分数，低于阈值自动迭代改进，适合生产环境中的QA流程。

## 12. 六标准提示词质量评分（Prompt Rubric）

**Prompt：**
```
Score this prompt against the 6-Criteria Rubric before running it:

Prompt: [待评估的提示词]

1. CLARITY: Is the task unambiguous? (1-5)
   Fast tell: Can I act on this without asking a follow-up?
2. SPECIFICITY: Are constraints and format defined? (1-5)
   Fast tell: Does the prompt name what to avoid?
3. CONTEXT: Is enough background provided? (1-5)
   Fast tell: Does the model know its audience?
4. ACTIONABILITY: Can the AI act on it immediately? (1-5)
   Fast tell: Is there a clear starting point?
5. MEASURABILITY: Would you know if the output is good? (1-5)
   Fast tell: Is there an explicit quality bar?
6. COMPLETENESS: Does it include all necessary elements? (1-5)

Total: /30. For scores below 4 on any criterion, suggest specific improvements.
```
> 来源：KeepMyPrompts — Score Any Prompt in 3 Minutes (2026)
> 用法：在运行提示词之前预检质量，而不是等输出结果不好了再责怪提示词。

## 13. 结构化思考框架（结构化 CoT）

**Prompt：**
```
Task: [需要推理的复杂任务]

请使用结构化思考框架逐步推理：

第一阶段 — 理解：用自己的话重述问题，明确输入和期望输出。
第二阶段 — 拆解：将问题分解为子问题，标注依赖关系。
第三阶段 — 分析：对每个子问题进行推理，标注置信度。
第四阶段 — 综合：合并各子问题的结论，形成最终答案。
第五阶段 — 验证：检查答案是否满足所有约束条件。

每个阶段都要显式输出，不要跳步。
```
> 来源：博客园 — 2026后端开发者进阶指南：提示词工程
> 用法：2026年升级版的 CoT，不再是简单的"一步步思考"，而是结构化的五阶段推理框架。
